1. Phishing

Phishing attacks trick users into giving away personal information by pretending to be legitimate organizations.

Example: Fake emails pretending to be from banks asking for login details.

2. Malware

Malware is malicious software designed to damage systems or steal information.

Types include:

  • Viruses
  • Worms
  • Trojans
  • Spyware

3. Ransomware

Ransomware encrypts files and demands payment to restore access.

Example: Hospitals or companies locked out of their systems until payment is made.

4. Social Engineering

Manipulating people into revealing confidential information.

5. DDoS Attacks

Distributed Denial of Service attacks overwhelm servers with traffic, causing websites to crash.

Awareness is the First Line of Defense.
