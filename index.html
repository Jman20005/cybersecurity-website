What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, networks, and data from malicious digital attacks. These attacks aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information, extort money, or interrupt business operations.

Why is Cybersecurity Important?

Today, almost everything is connected to the internet — banking, shopping, education, and communication. Without cybersecurity, personal data such as passwords, credit card details, and private messages could be stolen.

Who Needs Cybersecurity?

  • Individuals protecting personal information
  • Businesses protecting customer data
  • Governments protecting national security
  • Schools protecting student records

Key Goals of Cybersecurity

  • Confidentiality – Keeping data private
  • Integrity – Preventing unauthorized changes
  • Availability – Ensuring systems are accessible
